Why MissionControl

Built for finishing,
not just capturing.

Most tools optimize for inbox zero. MissionControl optimizes for one thing done well. Every pixel serves that goal.

🎯

One Mission at a Time

Select a task, start a session, and everything else fades away. The entire interface reorganizes around what you chose to focus on.

⏱️

Focus Sessions

Built-in session timer with pause, resume, and reset. Track elapsed time, see progress, and close the loop with session notes.

🪟

HUD Overlay

A translucent always-on-top window that shows your active mission and countdown. Expand it for full control, or keep it compact.

⚡

Instant Capture

Hit Ctrl+Shift+Space from anywhere to capture a thought. AI refines the title, and it lands in your inbox without breaking flow.

🧠

AI-Ready Architecture

Pluggable AI layer for title refinement, subtask suggestions, and clarifying questions. Works offline with mock, ready for any LLM.

🎨

Tokenized Theming

Four curated palettes powered by CSS custom properties. Every surface, shadow, and accent adapts instantly across all three windows.
